Lasagna

Description

This is where I put a long rambling story about a vacation to Tuscany and how this little old lady made the best lasagna I've ever eaten

This is where I put a long list of changes that I made to her recipe so that my 8 year-old would like it.

Don't forget the whole bunch of changes that I made because I couldn't bother to make it from scratch.

Ingredients

  • Stouffer's frozen lasagna
  • 1lb shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna according to package instructions
  2. Add all of the shredded cheese to the top of the lasagna and place back into the oven
  3. Remove once cheese is browned to your liking
